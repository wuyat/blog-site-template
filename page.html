About Me

Ever since I was a little kid, I have always loved and found inspiration in superheros. The main focus of my writing is the historical, theoretical, and philosophical aspects of superheros and their portrayal in various media, including film, games, and comics, particularly of Batman and Superman.

Educational Background

I graduated from the University of Texas at Austin as a Liberal Arts Honors student in May 2017 with a major in Classics/Latin and a minor in History. Having deeply studied Ancient Greek, Latin, and Egyptian languages, I possess a strong base of knowledge of grammatical, historical, and mythological topics. I use my understanding of these topics, as well as my passionate study of politics and science, to discuss and analyse superheros.

Main Superhero Interests

I particularly like to write about DC Comics, DC Films, DC Video Games, Batman, Superman, The Justice League, The Joker, The Martian Manhunter, and Shazam

Other Interests

Additionally, in my personal time, I enjoy gardening, cooking, and reading history books.
